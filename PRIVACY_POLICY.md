# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** November 19, 2025

## Introduction

"Norm: Habit tracker" ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use our mobile application ("App").

## Data Collection and Usage

**We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.**

*   **Local Storage:** All data you enter into the App (such as habit names, frequencies, and completion logs) is stored locally on your device using a local database. We do not have access to this data, and it is not transmitted to any external servers.
*   **No Account Required:** You can use the App without creating an account or providing any personal details.

## Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party services that collect data, such as analytics tools, advertising networks, or crash reporting services.

## Permissions

The App does not request any sensitive permissions (such as location, camera, or contacts) to function.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
